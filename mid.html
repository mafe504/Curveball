Developer Log: System Updates

Welcome to the enhanced version of the Curveball Simulator. Here are the key systematic upgrades implemented during this update cycle:

  • Dynamic Vulnerability Engine: Replaced generic "budget cut" scenarios with context-aware AI constraints targeting highly specific project materials and structural flaws.
  • Profile Calibration: Added user profile integration (Name, Age, Grade) to dynamically adjust AI difficulty, vocabulary, and crisis complexity based on the student's level.
  • 3-Trial Pivot Loop: Replaced instant acceptance with a 3-turn iteration loop, requiring strategic growth and forcing users to address compounding complications.
  • Cryptic Hint System: Shifted from full-sentence hints to a 2-4 raw keyword system to encourage independent problem-solving.
  • Rigorous Constraints: Enforced a 15-word minimum constraint on student submissions to demand deeper analytical thinking and explanation.
  • Reporting Suite: Added comprehensive HTML to PDF download and Print functionalities for the final evaluation report.
  • AI Boss Interrogation: Unlocked the AI Boss Feed, allowing users to actively interrogate the system about technical crisis parameters in real-time.
